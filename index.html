Doctrine

  1. Default bias: protect margins, invest in growth. Don't hoard cash.
  2. Judge channels and people by LTV:CAC, not feelings. Min 3:1, 5:1+ is great.
  3. Scale only when gross profit per client and payback are healthy. Cash from one client should fund the next in ~30 days.
  4. Revenue is vanity, profit is sanity, cash is reality. Cut anything that doesn't protect profit or get/keep clients.
  5. Invest in one-time builds (scripts, SOPs, automations) — high effort once, low effort forever.
